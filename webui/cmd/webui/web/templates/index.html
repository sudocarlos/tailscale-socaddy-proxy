About Tailrelay

Manage TCP relays and HTTPS proxies in one place. Tailrelay combines Tailscale, Caddy, and socat to expose local services securely to your Tailnet.

MagicDNS Requirement

Enable MagicDNS in the Tailscale DNS settings. Tailnets created on or after October 20, 2022 have MagicDNS enabled by default.
